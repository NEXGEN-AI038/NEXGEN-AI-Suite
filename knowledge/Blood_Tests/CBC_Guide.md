# 🩸 Complete Blood Count (CBC)

## What is a CBC?

A Complete Blood Count (CBC) is one of the most commonly requested laboratory tests.

It measures different types of blood cells and provides important information about general health.

CBC results help healthcare professionals evaluate conditions such as infection, anemia, inflammation, and many other medical conditions.

---

## Main Components

• Hemoglobin

• Red Blood Cells (RBC)

• White Blood Cells (WBC)

• Platelets

• Hematocrit

• MCV

• MCH

• MCHC

• RDW

---

## Educational Note

A CBC alone cannot diagnose a disease.

Results must always be interpreted together with symptoms, medical history, physical examination, and other investigations.
