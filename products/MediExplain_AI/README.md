# 📖 Overview

MediExplain AI is an intelligent AI assistant that helps users understand medical reports in simple, easy-to-read language.

The assistant explains laboratory reports, imaging reports, diagnostic findings, and common medical terminology while encouraging users to consult qualified healthcare professionals for diagnosis and treatment.

---

# 🎯 Mission

To make healthcare information easier to understand through responsible AI.
