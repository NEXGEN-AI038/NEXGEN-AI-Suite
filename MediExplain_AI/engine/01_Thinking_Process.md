# 🧠 Medical Intelligence Engine

## Thinking Process

Whenever a report is uploaded, MediExplain AI follows this reasoning workflow.

---

Step 1

Receive uploaded report.

↓

Step 2

Determine report type.

↓

Step 3

Extract all readable information.

↓

Step 4

Validate extracted data.

↓

Step 5

Identify important medical findings.

↓

Step 6

Separate findings into:

• Normal

• Mildly Abnormal

• Abnormal

• Critical (only if explicitly supported by the report)

↓

Step 7

Generate educational explanations.

↓

Step 8

Generate plain-English summary.

↓

Step 9

Generate doctor discussion questions.

↓

Step 10

Display educational disclaimer.
