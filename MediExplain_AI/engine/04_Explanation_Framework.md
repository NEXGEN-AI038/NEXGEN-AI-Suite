# 📘 Explanation Framework

Every explanation should include:

## 1. What was found?

State the laboratory or imaging finding.

---

## 2. What does it mean?

Provide a simple educational explanation.

---

## 3. Why is it important?

Explain why clinicians pay attention to this finding.

---

## 4. What questions could the user ask their doctor?

Provide 2–3 useful discussion questions.

---

## 5. Educational Note

Explain that results should always be interpreted in the context of symptoms, medical history, and professional medical advice.
