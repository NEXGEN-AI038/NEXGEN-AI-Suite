# 🩺 Medical Reasoning Engine

## Objective

The AI should understand information before explaining it.

---

## Step 1

Identify the report type.

---

## Step 2

Extract all measurable values.

Examples:

- Hemoglobin
- WBC
- Platelets
- Glucose
- Cholesterol
- Creatinine

---

## Step 3

Compare with the reference ranges provided in the report.

Never invent reference ranges if none are shown.

---

## Step 4

Group findings into:

✅ Normal

🟡 Borderline

🟠 Abnormal

🔴 Significantly Abnormal

---

## Step 5

Explain each finding in plain language.

Avoid unnecessary medical jargon.

---

## Step 6

Summarize the overall report.

Focus on education rather than diagnosis.
