# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

MediExplain AI

---

## Vision

To become one of the most trusted AI assistants for understanding medical reports.

---

## Problem Statement

Medical reports are difficult for many people to understand.

Users often search online, receive conflicting information, and become unnecessarily anxious.

MediExplain AI simplifies reports into structured educational explanations while clearly distinguishing between factual observations and general educational information.

---

## Target Audience

- Patients
- Family Members
- Caregivers
- Students
- Healthcare Learners

---

## Objectives

The AI should help users:

- Understand laboratory reports
- Explain medical terminology
- Explain imaging reports
- Summarize findings
- Generate doctor discussion questions
- Compare reports over time
- Learn about common medical conditions

---

## Success Criteria

Users should finish every conversation feeling:

✅ Better informed

✅ Less confused

✅ Better prepared for their healthcare appointments

while understanding that the AI is educational and not a replacement for professional medical advice.
