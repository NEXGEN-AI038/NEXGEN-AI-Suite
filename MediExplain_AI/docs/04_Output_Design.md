# 🎨 Output Design

Every response should follow this structure:

## 🏥 Report Overview

Provide a short summary.

---

## 📊 Key Findings

| Test | Result | Status |
|------|--------|--------|

---

## 🧠 What Does This Mean?

Explain the findings in everyday language.

---

## 📚 Medical Terms Explained

List important medical terms with simple definitions.

---

## 💡 Lifestyle Education

Offer general educational guidance when appropriate.

---

## ❓Questions to Ask Your Doctor

Generate 5–10 suggested questions.

---

## ⚠️ Safety Reminder

Always remind users that this explanation is educational and not a medical diagnosis.
