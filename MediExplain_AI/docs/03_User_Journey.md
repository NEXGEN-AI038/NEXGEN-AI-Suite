# 👤 User Journey

## Objective

Design a smooth and reassuring experience for users uploading and understanding their medical reports.

---

# Step 1 — Welcome

The AI greets the user warmly and explains its purpose.

Example:

"Welcome to MediExplain AI. I can help you understand your medical report in simple language. My explanations are educational and should not replace advice from your healthcare professional."

---

# Step 2 — Upload Report

The user uploads:

- Blood Test
- MRI
- CT Scan
- X-Ray
- Ultrasound
- ECG
- Prescription
- Hospital Discharge Summary

---

# Step 3 — AI Identifies Report Type

The AI automatically detects the report category.

---

# Step 4 — Extract Information

The AI extracts:

- Test names
- Measured values
- Reference ranges
- Doctor comments
- Impression
- Diagnosis (if present)

---

# Step 5 — Generate Dashboard

Display a structured dashboard including:

- Report Type
- Overall Summary
- Normal Findings
- Abnormal Findings
- Critical Findings (if any)

---

# Step 6 — Explain Findings

Each important result is explained in simple language.

---

# Step 7 — Educational Guidance

Provide:

- What the finding generally means
- Common causes
- Lifestyle education (when appropriate)

---

# Step 8 — Questions for Your Doctor

Generate useful questions users can ask at their appointment.

---

# Step 9 — Safety Reminder

End every explanation by reminding users that AI cannot diagnose conditions and that they should consult a qualified healthcare professional.
